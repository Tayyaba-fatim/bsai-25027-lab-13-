Our Story

Welcome to EcoHome Essentials! We believe that sustainable living shouldn't mean sacrificing style or convenience. Our journey started in 2020 when our founder, Sarah, struggled to find high-quality, genuinely eco-friendly household products that actually worked. Frustrated by "greenwashing" and single-use plastics, she set out to create a solution: durable, beautifully designed products that make sustainable choices simple and rewarding for modern homes.

What We Do

We offer a curated selection of reusable kitchen items, non-toxic cleaning supplies, and ethically sourced home decor.
Our products are designed to solve everyday problems while minimizing your environmental footprint. We're proud to offer products that are: Ethically Sourced: We partner with suppliers who prioritize fair labor practices and transparent supply chains.
Built to Last: We focus on durable materials to combat the disposable culture. Certified Green: Our cleaning products are certified by the EPA's Safer Choice program.

Our Mission and Values

Mission: To make sustainable living accessible and stylish for everyone. Values: Transperency: We're honest about our sourcing, our challenges, and our impact.
