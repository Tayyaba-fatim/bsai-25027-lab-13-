Product Types

Apparel:
This is the most common product, with subcategories including men's wear, ladies' wear, children's wear, ethnic wear, formal and casual wear, sportswear, and outerwear like jackets and coats.
Fabrics and materials:
You can sell raw materials like cotton, silk, wool, or polyester fabrics, as well as other textile products like yarn and fiber.
Accessories:
Garment accessories such as buttons, zippers, labels, patches, and ribbons are essential and can also be sold as a product line.
Home textiles:
Some businesses include products like blankets, which have seen a recent surge in popularity.

Product Types

Apparel:
Natural materials are prized for their softness, breathability, and hypoallergenic properties, making them a top choice for everyday wear, sleepwear, and sensitive skin.
Cotton:
The most popular fabric for kids' clothing due to its softness, breathability, durability, and easy care.
Organic Cotton:
Grown without pesticides, making it a safer and eco-friendly option for sensitive skin.
Look for GOTS or OEKO-TEX certifications.
